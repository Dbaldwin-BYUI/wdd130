Contact Us

This website is for anyone who has interest in gardening. Whether you are new to gardening or have years of experience we have a gardening club they can join and share ideas and tips on gardening. Our planner has a common list of fruits and vegetables along with data for each on planting, watering and harvesting. This is a great guide for those want to look up how much soil area is needed, water to use, and how many days till harvesting for many different fruits and vegetables. We also have a monthly newsletter that you can sign up to receive via e-mail. This newsletter has many articles on gardening keeping you up to date on the vast array of gardening subjects. Our website, newsletter and gardening club are user friendly to navigate, easy to sign up for and free to use.
